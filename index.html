ABOUT US


Mosie Selaelo Velly (PTY) LTD is a black-owned construction company that embraces the principles of broad-based empowerment. We employ youth and women with the aim of improving the quality of life in Limpopo and beyond. Our vision is to become a reliable and leading provider of construction services, catering to the needs of our clients in both provincial and local government sectors.
